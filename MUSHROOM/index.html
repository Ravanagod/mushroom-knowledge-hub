What are Mushrooms?

Mushrooms are fungi, not plants. They grow from spores and play a vital role in ecosystems by decomposing organic matter.

Why Mushrooms Are Important

  • High nutritional value
  • Medicinal properties
  • Eco-friendly cultivation
  • Income source for farmers
