Example: Fly Agaric

  • Scientific Name: Amanita muscaria
  • Common Names: Fly agaric
  • Edibility: ‚ùå Poisonous
  • Habitat: Forests, near trees (pine, birch)

Identification Features

  • Bright red cap with white spots
  • White gills
  • White stem with ring

Toxic Effects

  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Hallucinations
  • Dizziness and confusion
  • Severe cases can be fatal

Common Dangerous Look-alikes

  • Young edible mushrooms (button stage)
  • Other Amanita species

Safety Guidelines

  • Never eat wild mushrooms
  • Do not rely on color or smell
  • Teach children mushroom safety
  • Consult agricultural or forest experts
